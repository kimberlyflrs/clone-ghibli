Studio Ghibili is a Japanese film animation studio founded in 1985. The studio is headed by Hayao Miyazaki along with Isao Takahata. Several anime features made by the studio have won the Animage Anime Grand Prix award including Castle in the Sky, My Neighbor Totoro, and Kiki's Delivery Service. Spirited Away won the Oscar for the Best Animated Feature and became the first aime to win an Academy Award.
